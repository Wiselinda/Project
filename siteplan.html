Website Plan for DedeGlamour

Site Name

DedeGlamour: Your Premier Destination for Stylish Wigs and Hair Accessories

The name was chosen to reflect the brand's focus on glamour, style, and high-quality hair products.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the DedeGlamour website is to provide an online platform for showcasing and selling a wide range of high-quality 100% human wigs and hair accessories. The site will offer a pleasant shopping experience with detailed product descriptions, styling tips, and customer reviews to help buyers make informed decisions.

Scenarios

  • What are the best wigs for people with sensitive scalps?
  • How can I take care of my new wig to ensure it lasts a long time?

Color Schema

The color scheme for the DedeGlamour website will be as follows:

  • #d62828 - Used for headings, buttons, and important accents.
  • #333333 - Used for text and navigation elements.
  • #f5f5f5 - Used for background color.

Typography

  • Arial, sans-serif - Used for body text and general content.
  • Georgia, serif - Used for headings to give a touch of elegance.

Planned Site Content

  • Homepage: Features welcome message, featured products, promotions, and navigation links.
  • Shop: Main storefront for browsing products by category, brand, or price range with detailed descriptions and images.
  • Style Tips and Guides: Resources for choosing and caring for wigs, including articles, video tutorials, and FAQs.
  • Customer Reviews and Testimonials: Page for customer feedback to build trust and credibility.
  • Contact Us: Contact information, including email, phone, social media links, and a contact form.
